Google Form + Sheet setup — Inbound Enquiries 2026
==================================================

1. Google Drive
   - Create folder: Lead Intake/
   - Create spreadsheet: Inbound Enquiries 2026 (inside that folder)

2. Google Form (forms.google.com)
   - Title: Bespoke AI — Consulting Enquiry
   - Description: Structured intake for regulated-sector automation and architecture engagements.
   - Link responses to: Inbound Enquiries 2026 spreadsheet

   Fields (required unless noted):
   - Full Name          (Short answer)
   - Business Email     (Short answer, response validation: Email)
   - Company            (Short answer)
   - Job Title          (Short answer)
   - Company Size       (Dropdown)
       • 1–50 employees
       • 51–200 employees
       • 201–1,000 employees
       • 1,000+ employees
   - Automation Problems (Paragraph)
       Label: What problems are you looking to solve through automation?

3. Branding (Form → Customize theme)
   - Header: dark image or colour #0f1a2b
   - Background: #152238 or #1f3049
   - Accent: #3b82f6 (primary), #06b6d4 (secondary)
   - Upload Bespoke AI / site logo if available

4. Apps Script (on the spreadsheet)
   - Open Inbound Enquiries 2026 → Extensions → Apps Script
   - Paste inbound-enquiries.gs → Save
   - Run setupSheetHeaders once (authorize)
   - Triggers → Add trigger:
       Function: onFormSubmit
       Event: From spreadsheet → On form submit

5. Form URL (live)
   - https://forms.gle/HCQSAhwR9JJpygxb9
   - Wired in index.html SITE.googleFormUrl and lead-intake outreach snippets
