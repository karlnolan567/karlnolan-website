Privacy Notice

Bespoke Core AI Engineering (Limerick, Ireland) processes contact details to respond to business enquiries and schedule discovery calls. Lawful basis: legitimate interest in operating the business and, where applicable, pre-contractual steps at your request.

Discovery call bookings are submitted via our booking page, stored in Google Calendar, and confirmed by email through Gmail (Google Ireland Limited / Google LLC act as processors for calendar and email delivery). A copy of the confirmation is sent to our business inbox. We do not sell personal data.

The Ask BCAI chat sends your messages to our automation server and an AI provider (OpenRouter) to generate replies — please do not submit sensitive personal data in chat.

Retention: enquiry and scheduling details are kept for up to 24 months after our last contact, then deleted unless a longer period is required for an active engagement or legal obligation.

For access, correction, or erasure, email privacy@ai-development.ie — do not resubmit personal data via the discovery form for deletion requests.
