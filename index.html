Supplier Verification for Regulated Supply Chains

Challenge
A compliance platform needed to verify suppliers using websites with no simple data feeds.
Approach
Automated website collection and document reading, augmented with human review where required for audit trails.
Outcome

Zero-Downtime Migration

Updating a Large Legacy System

Challenge
One large old application was slowing releases and struggling at busy times.
Approach
Split into smaller parts, moved to Google Cloud, and switched over without downtime.
Outcome
Releases went from monthly to weekly; production incidents dropped within six months.
Human-in-the-Loop Documents

Invoice & Document Processing

Challenge
Finance teams spent too much time pulling data from invoices and attachments in email.
Approach
Automated document intake, field extraction, validation, and hand-off to finance systems — with human review on exceptions. Exception alerts can be routed through APIs and webhooks to Slack, WhatsApp, or Telegram.
Outcome
Roughly 40 hours per month of manual data entry removed; receipt-to-record time cut by more than half.
