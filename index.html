Audit-Ready Automation

Supplier Verification for Regulated Supply Chains

Challenge
A compliance platform needed to verify suppliers using websites with no simple data feeds.
Approach
Automated website collection and document reading, augmented with human review where required for audit trails.
Outcome
Manual research per supplier cut from hours to minutes; 100% of outputs human-reviewed until the client signed off on full automation.
Smart Inbox Automation

Customer Support Triage & Draft Replies

Challenge
Support inboxes were flooded — urgent and frustrated messages sat alongside routine enquiries, and every reply was written from scratch.
Approach
Automated email intake, urgency and sentiment triage, and AI-drafted replies saved to drafts for review. Angry or critical messages trigger instant alerts via Slack, WhatsApp, or Telegram.
Outcome
Urgent messages flagged within minutes; routine enquiries drafted same day. Nothing sends without human approval — the team reviews drafts, not blank pages.
Human-in-the-Loop Documents

Invoice & Document Processing

Challenge
Finance teams spent too much time pulling data from invoices and attachments in email.
Approach
Automated document intake, field extraction, validation, and hand-off to finance systems — with human review on exceptions. Exception alerts can be routed through APIs and webhooks to Slack, WhatsApp, or Telegram.
Outcome
Roughly 40 hours per month of manual data entry removed; receipt-to-record time cut by more than half.
